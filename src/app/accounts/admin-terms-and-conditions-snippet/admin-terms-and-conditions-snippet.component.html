Study Objectives

The objective of this work is to develop educational software that helps students and course administrators to automatically generate and interpret feedback from assignment submissions in a course.

Procedure: Software Feedback Usefulness (Total Expected Time: 1 hour)

  1. A student researcher will visit your class without your presence to explain the study and the consent form to the students.
  2. As part of the course, your students will work on assignments and submit them as usual. Nothing will change from standard procedures.
  3. You and the students can use the software as much or as little as you see fit. There are many educational activities available in the software that you can use throughout the semester.
  4. At the end of the course, you will be asked to complete a short questionnaire that helps us identify what was useful for you and what changes you would like to see in the future.

Privacy and Confidentiality

Questionnaires and computer logs are anonymous. Names of participants will be stored separately from information collected. Your name will not be associated in any way with the information collected about you or your usage of the computer during the study. Furthermore, because the interest of this study is to identify average responses and behavior of the entire group of participants, you will not be identified in any way in any written reports of this research.

Since we are only interested in average responses and behaviors, reports about individual students will not be produced or released. You may choose to provide a bonus mark in the course as an incentive for students to participate in this study. If you choose to do so, we will provide you with a list of consented participants. If you choose not to do so, then student participation will have no impact on their course grade. Meanwhile, if any student decided not to participate in the study, they can be provided with an alternative question for the additional bonus mark. If you decided to not offer an alternative bonus question, all bonus marks for the participation will be cancelled.

Participants have a right to access their own results as long as their names and numeric tags are still available. The data obtained from both components of the study will be retained for at least 5 years after publication in secured, electronic form, which only researchers associated with this research will have access to. No audio or video tapes will be used. General results of the research study may be available through publications. A summary of the results will be made available to participants upon request.
